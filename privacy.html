Privacy Policy

Last updated:
This policy covers the apps published by Vuolt, including Call My Meeting, Missed Flight, ReelRoutes, One Second Challenge, Borderchain, Breakup Counter, German A1 Quiz, and Memory Board Game. Review this against each app's actual behaviour before publishing, and update if any app changes what it collects.

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information our apps do — and do not — collect.

Information we collect

Our apps are designed to work without accounts and without collecting personal information. We do not ask for your name, email, contacts, or location, and we do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

  • No personal data: we don't collect identifiable information about you.
  • On-device data: things like your scores, progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.
  • No advertising or tracking: our apps do not contain third-party ads and do not track you across apps or websites.

Calendar access (Call My Meeting)

Call My Meeting asks for permission to read your calendar so it can set a local alarm before each meeting. Your calendar stays private:

  • Read on-device only: calendar events are read on your device to work out when to ring. They are never uploaded, sent to us, or shared with anyone.
  • No copies kept: the app does not store your events on a server. It only keeps what it needs on your device to schedule upcoming alarms.
  • Alarms are local: alarms and reminders are scheduled and fire entirely on your device.
  • Optional notifications: if you allow notifications, they are used only as an occasional on-device nudge to reopen the app. There is no push server.

You can turn Calendar, Alarms, or Notifications access on or off at any time in your device Settings.

Platform-provided data

Apple (the App Store) and Google (Google Play) may collect standard, aggregated, and anonymised information — such as crash reports, download counts, and basic usage analytics — under their own privacy policies. We may view this information in summary form to fix bugs and improve our apps. It is governed by:

  • Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google's Privacy Policy

Purchases & support

If you choose to support us via “Buy me a coffee,” that transaction is handled entirely by Buy Me a Coffee on their own website and under their own privacy policy. Our apps do not process or store any payment information.

Children's privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we'll address it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest revision.
