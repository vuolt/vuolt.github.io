Support

Need help with a Vuolt app, found a bug, or have a feature idea? I'd love to hear from you.

✉ Email vuolt@outlook.com

Vuolt is a one-person studio, so replies come straight from me. I read every message and try to get back as soon as I can.

When you write in, it helps to include the app name (for example Call My Meeting), your device and OS version, and a short description of what happened.

Call My Meeting

Alarms not firing? Make sure Calendar and Alarms are allowed for the app in Settings, and that the app has been opened at least once so it can schedule ahead. For anything else, just email me.

Route Reel

Photos not showing up on the map? Route Reel places stops using the GPS location saved inside each photo, so make sure you grant Photos access (with location) and that the photos you pick actually have location data — screenshots and edited copies often don't. If a trip looks empty, try photos taken on the device with location switched on. For anything else, just email me.
